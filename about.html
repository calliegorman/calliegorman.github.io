Hi there, I’m Callie.
I am a UX designer passionate about creating incredible user experiences.

My education in psychology and digital marketing experience have prepared me well for a career in UX design. Early on, I was a marketing events manager whose favorite task was to ensure each point of a guest’s experience was flawless. It was the first time I realized I love creating delightful experiences for people. In more recent digital marketing roles, I became familiar with the importance of design through reach projects where I worked alongside the design and developer teams to improve engagement and conversion rates on our web pages.

In 2017, I followed a dream I had for years and took a leave of absence from work to travel solo for several months. During this time, I thought about the things during my career that I loved doing, most notably creating these experiences for users. When I returned, I took a chance and enrolled in Bloc’s UX/UI Design Course and never looked back. I still love hypothesizing and testing as I did in my early psychology years, and enjoy tackling design problems with a team to continuously improve user experiences.

Some of my favorite hobbies are tracking my travels via my GoPro, hiking challenging trails, and making music. Currently open to new opportunities, so please get in touch!
