About Me


Hi there, I’m Callie.
I am a UX designer passionate about creating incredible user experiences.

My education in psychology and work as a digital strategist guided me to a career in design, where I can now conduct user research, create intuitive and user-centered designs, and increase user engagement rates to meet business goals.

Early on, I was a marketing events manager whose favorite task was to ensure each point of a guest’s experience was flawless. It was the first time I realized I love creating delightful experiences for people. In a more recent digital strategy role, I increased engagement and conversion rates on poor performing web pages and worked in an Agile environment improving the user experience of the live chat feature on ibm.com

In 2017, I followed a dream I had for years and took a leave of absence from work to travel solo for several months. During this time, I thought about the things during my career that I loved doing, most notably creating better digital experiences for users. When I returned, I took a chance and enrolled in Bloc’s UX/UI Design Course and never looked back. I still love hypothesizing and testing as I did in my early psychology years, and enjoy tackling design problems with a team to continuously improve user experiences.

Some of my favorite hobbies are tracking my travels via my GoPro, hiking challenging trails, and making music. Currently open to new opportunities, so please get in touch!
